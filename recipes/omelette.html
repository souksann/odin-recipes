Omelette

How to make the an amazing omelette.

Cooking can be daunting at times. Or it can be simple. For me, I try to stick to simplicity, and an omelette is as simple as it gets.

An omelette's simplicity is within the ingredients. You only need two things and that's it. Easy, simple, and fast.

Here's what you'll need.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs into a smooth mixture.
  3. Add butter to non-stick saucepan at medium to low heat.
  4. Add whisked eggs to the saucepan when bubbles start to show in the butter.
  5. Move eggs to cover the full surface of the saucepan by stirring and scraping the eggs.
  6. Gently begin to roll over the omelette into a fold.
  7. When fully cooked. Remove the omelette from the saucepan onto a plate.
  8. Enjoy.
